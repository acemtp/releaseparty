FAQ

Why should I come?

  • to have a memorable experience
  • to show to your peers what amazing things you can do in only a week end
  • to meet and talk with awesome people
  • to break out of your comfort zone and learn new things
  • to release something!

Where ? When? And how do I get a ticket?

It'll take place in Paris in November. Tickets are not for sale at the moment.

Register and you'll be the first to get notified!

Will there be prizes?

Our sponsors will gift prizes for selected completed achievements. We don't recommend choosing you achievements based solely on the prizes though ;)

How do achievements & badges work?

Since the Release Party is about DOing stuff, you'll get credit for it! We have listed a lot of achievements that can be done during the week-end: Opensourcer, Releaser, Hardware, Game, Web, Sponsor, Mentor, FastCompo... (full list will be available on D-day).

Each achievement will be rewarded with a badge. Choose the achievements you want to do, and get the badges!

I'm a game designer or product manager, can I join?

The event focus on creating a project, if you can do something like programming, designing, drawing (even if you are a novice) just subscribe under this category.

Your talent for game design or product management will be a plus for your team.

NB: if you don't have DOer skill but still think that you can contribute to the Release Party, send a mail to hi@releaseparty.net explaining us why you want to come and how you can help a team. Show us you are a DOer!

I'm not a bizdev, can I win?

First of all, it's not a contest: every participant will be a winner!

So don't expect to launch a startup or go to SF. You don't have a business model? That's the point!

What are the Fast contest?

Because it's too easy to release a project in 40 hours, we'll challenge you with fast contests during the week end (1 hour long). Accept them and show us that nothing can scare you. Fast contest will be of course rewarded with badges.

Teams & project brainstorming

One we announce the theme, you'll have 30mn to think of a cool project to work on during the Release Party (you can brainstorm alone or in teams, as you wish). All the people who have ideas will the have 15 to 30 seconds to present them to everybody. No need to prepare some slides, just explain what you want to do, if you are looking for people (and which skills)...

You'll then get one hour either join or create a team. And after that, the DOing will begin!

Any questions?

You have a question that is not in this FAQ? Drop us an email and it'll be a pleasure to answer you.
