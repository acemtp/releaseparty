Release Party is about building and sharing cool stuff over the week-end. Whether you are an expert or novice, as long as you plan to do & release something during the week end, join us for a memorable experience.

By DOers for DOers

Artist, musician, hardware hacker, programmer... as long as you want to DO something Release Party is the event for you.

Pure pleasure? Just for fun?

No ranks, no judges, no slides, no final pitch. You are here to create awesome things.

Collaboration & creativity

Meet new people & create a geniune team to share and invent the weirdest, coolest, most creative things.
